***Remote Finance and Accounting Roles***

    Core Accounting Roles

  • Remote Bookkeeper: Manage daily transactions, reconcile accounts, and maintain ledgers.
  • Staff Accountant: Handle journal entries, financial statements, and month-end close.
  • Accounts Payable/Receivable Clerk: Process invoices, track payments, and manage cash flow.

    • Financial Management & Strategy

  • Financial Analyst: Budgeting, forecasting, and performance analysis.
  • FP&A Analyst: Long-term financial planning and scenario modeling.
  • Controller: Oversee accounting operations and financial reporting.
  • Fractional CFO/VP of Finance: Strategic financial leadership (part-time or contract).

    • Tax & Compliance

  • Tax Accountant/Preparer: Prepare tax returns and ensure compliance.
  • Compliance Officer: Monitor regulatory adherence (e.g., GAAP, IRS).
  • Internal Auditor: Assess internal controls and risk management.

    • Payroll & Operations

  • Payroll Specialist: Manage payroll processing and compliance.
  • Cost Accountant: Analyze production costs and profitability (if applicable).

    • Advisory & Consulting

  • Financial Consultant: Provide expertise on mergers, process optimization, or software implementation.

    • Technology & Systems

  • ERP/Accounting Software Specialist: Manage QuickBooks, Xero,
  • Financial Data Analyst: Use BI tools (Tableau, Power BI) for data-driven insights.

    • Specialized Roles

  • Credit Analyst: Evaluate client credit risk and repayment capacity.
  • Technical Writer (Finance): Create guides on accounting processes or tools .
